Pre-Requisite Knowledge

This article assumes you know the concept and implementation of coordinate compression and the concept of a segment tree with lazy propagation.

Introduction

Coordinate compression is a very useful technique in competitive programming but the generalization to handling operations on ranges may not be entirely obvious. We examine a problem that can be solved using range operations and coordinate compression and discuss the correct way to combine the ideas.
